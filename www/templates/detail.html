TakeCharge is an autism assistance app built for the 2017 Spark for Autism app development contest. The goal was to design and build an app to help those with autism (and their parents/teaches/etc) track patterns in their mood and manage their autism-related issues. We set requirements for the project based on user research done by the Spark organization. The app is built with Ionic Framework, an app development system built on Angular 2 and Typescript. In our team of four students, my role was UI designer, front-end developer, and project manager. Our team used agile software development process.

Funk Water is an exploration of typography as brand. The project purpose was to create an effective, memorable and readable mark using purely typographic elements. The Funk Water mark communicates fun and youth, with a funky/jazzy vibe that matches the product.

Domain of the Gods examines the expressive aspect of different typefaces. The goal of the project was to create illustrations and marry they to typefaces that express their character. Then these type-image marriages were integrated into editorial-style spreads to demonstrate scale change and effective layouts.

Red Thread by Roadkill Annies is an punk/alternative rock album. The goal was to communicate the character of the band and the music through bright, contrasting colors and dynamic texture and movement.

Desire & Innocence is a design of an excerpt from Bruce Mau's Incomplete Manifesto for Growth. This goal of this project was to develop an image manipulation, a vector illustration, two flat colors, and three textual elements (title, number, and subtitle) and create three dynamic layouts. One layout is image-dominant, one is text-dominant, and the last is color-dominant. My concept explores the duality of desire and innocence in youth expression of sexuality.
